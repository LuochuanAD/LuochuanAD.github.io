Hi, My name is Louis Luo. I am currently based in Tokyo, primarily working on iOS application development, and in recent years, I have also been engaged in developing applications that leverage generative AI.

I have over 10 years of experience in iOS development, comprising approximately 10 years with Objective-C and about 4 years with Swift.

In my iOS development work, I go beyond mere implementation; I communicate directly with clients to clarify requirements and conduct user research. I provide proposals from both technical and product perspectives and lead the overall direction of the applications. I handle the entire lifecycle—from initial planning and UI/UX design to development, testing, and release—and have successfully launched multiple iOS apps.

I have approximately five years of development experience using Python and web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).

In recent years, alongside iOS development, I have been working on applications that utilize generative AI technology. I have independently designed and developed multiple AI applications and AI agents leveraging LLMs and RAG, building systems that incorporate RAG infrastructure, memory management, vector search, and multi-agent coordination. I also engage in AI application development with a focus on real-world operation, including building web applications with FastAPI and Next.js, setting up development and deployment environments using Docker and AWS, and designing knowledge-update pipelines.

My strength lies in developing systems that enhance user value by integrating new technologies into products, all while leveraging the solid iOS development expertise I have cultivated over the years.
