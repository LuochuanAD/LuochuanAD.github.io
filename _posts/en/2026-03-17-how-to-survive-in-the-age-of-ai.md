## How to Survive in the AI Era?

In the AI era, job displacement is inevitable.

This means your income source will no longer be stable, and your monthly disposable income will fluctuate.

In this transformation, what truly determines your survival is your risk resilience:

- Your personal debt load,
- Your minimum monthly living expenses,
- Your physical and mental health condition.

As most middle-class people are treated equally by AI, the gaps will widen:

- Top talent will stand out,
- The middle class will face severe pressure,
- The lower class will struggle even more,
- Those who provide survival and security for others will become increasingly important,
- Prices will rise, making human survival more difficult,
- Large companies will evolve into super-platforms, while small companies will be broken down into OPC companies or small teams,
- Monopolies will become more severe,
- Countries with excessive currency printing will face bubbles,
- Chaos will increase.

The only hope for ordinary people is: **that governments worldwide leverage AI to lead humanity in exploring space or the oceans to acquire more extra resources; only by continuously expanding resource boundaries can competition avoid becoming a zero-sum game.**
